🏙️ I analyzed my own city — Bhopal — through data.

Bhopal is officially a Smart City under India's Smart Cities Mission. But what does the data actually say?

I built a complete data analysis project to audit Bhopal's Smart City performance across 4 dimensions:

🏛️ Smart City Delivery
🌿 Environment & Lakes
🏥 Healthcare Access
📋 Citizen Grievances

🔍 Key Findings:
• Only 20% of Smart City projects fully delivered despite 53% budget utilized
• Winter AQI averages 174 — classified as Poor air quality
• City has only 1.78 hospital beds per 1000 people (WHO recommends 5)
• 199K out of 560K citizen complaints remain unresolved

🛠️ Tools Used:
→ Python 
→ PostgreSQL 
→ KNIME 
→ Power BI 

📊 The dashboard includes a ward-level drill down, lake water quality trends, AQI seasonal analysis and a full insights & recommendations page.
